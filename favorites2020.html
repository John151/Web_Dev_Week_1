Some of my favorite things

By John Cokins

Favorite Movie

My favorite movie is Monsters Inc. I think Pixar movies are the best, they're simple stories and really funny. This movie is a comedy about a world of monsters who get their energy from children's screams. It follows a "scarer" Sully Sullivan voiced by John Goodman band his technician Mike Wazowski voiced by Billy Crystal. The funny twist is that monsters think humans are toxic and are deathly afraid of the children they scare.
